What is It?

Ordering food as a group can be a tough task. With everyone having their own preferences, pleasing everyone with one reasonable sized order can seem almost impossible. Enter the Pizza Picker! Your order is now as easy as listing everyone's usernames! When the group is set, the picker uses each user's preferences to design an order that is perfect for everyone. Less time. Less hassle. More pizza!
